Communication Design Fundamentals

Spring 2009

Communication Design Fundamentals (CDF) was a one-semester, project-based design course. Throughout the semester, we explored the basics of communication design, from typography to color theory and the design process.


CD Cover - Typographic Music

For this assignment (and final project) I designed a CD insert - front, back, insert and the CD itself. I chose mine to be Electric Feel, an awesome song by the band MGMT. The letterforms on the cover are original and made in Adobe Illustrator.
